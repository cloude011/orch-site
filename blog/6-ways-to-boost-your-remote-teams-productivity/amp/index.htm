6 Ways to Boost Your Remote Team's Productivity

Sabina V - 05 Nov 2024

Remote work demands more than just getting tasks done — it's about maintaining smooth workflows and creating a unified space where teams can collaborate, communicate, and execute efficiently. To be productive, one needs a responsive workplace to start with. 

This is where Orchestra steps in. An all-in-one workspace made to boost team collaboration and productivity by integrating communication and management tools into one seamless platform.

This article explores six ways Orchestra can complement your productivity while working remotely.

Get all the context in one workspace

One of the biggest productivity killers in remote teams is app-switching: when you jump from messenger to task trackers and vice versa. You're just wasting time trying to complete tasks and not lose context. Orchestra addresses this challenge with communication and management tools within a single platform, letting your team focus on work tasks rather than dealing with tool chaos.

Key features

  • Customized chat boards. Streamline your chat list and personalize your work feed. The customized board displays chats associated with particular projects and tasks, allowing you to monitor statuses. This way, context and messages are never lost.
  • Seamless communication & task workflow. Endless comments in task trackers, separate threads, or switching to private messages create FOMO and make you lose data. From now on, address all urgent matters in a topic-based space without losing track of the task.
💡
Pro tip. Tired of writing down important tasks and then remembering them? Convert chat messages into tasks in one click. With Orchestra, it's that simple.

Communicate in real-time

Here-and-now communication is what drives projects forward. The delays caused by setting up external tools like Zoom can disrupt momentum and slow progress. With real-time communication at your fingertips, Orchestra helps teams stay aligned, avoid misunderstandings, and move faster.

Key features

  • Built-in voice & video calls. Remote team members can quickly jump on a call for face-to-face discussions. Less time is spent trying to get on a Zoom call, and more on brainstorming sessions by starting virtual conversations.
  • Autosaved video recordings & meeting notes. Ensure that all your team members are informed and record your meeting for those who couldn't attend. Orchestra has your back on this and will automatically share your recording and meeting notes in the chat.

Increase task management transparency

Without transparent task tracking remote teams hardly succeed — deadlines can be missed, and responsibilities are forgotten. Orchestra acknowledges the pain and provides a solution for assigning, monitoring, and managing tasks.

Key features

  • Fields in chat sidebars. This feature adds structure to discussions by allowing you to specify any project or task attribute. The best part? All team members have access to the same context, ensuring transparency and alignment across the board.
  • Projects section. Each chat has its dedicated space in the Projects section, enabling you to create tailored management workflows for every department and requirement. You can view projects as a list or a Kanban board, with powerful options to filter, group, sort, search, and customize your workflow to fit your unique needs.

Unify knowledge hub

Besides communication tools like chat, having a readily accessible knowledge base is what ensures everyone stays on the same page. Orchestra gets that. Here're features to keep your team aligned and connected through shared knowledge.

Key features

  • Docs section for online collaboration. There's no need to leave the platform for document creation or editing. Orchestra's docs section is feature-rich yet simple and allows one to create and collaborate on documents without switching tools.
  • Pinning files & documents. Keep important documents at your fingertips by pinning them to specific tasks. Access is almost instant — just head to the project chat, open the sidebar, and your files are right there, ready when you need them.

Keep deadlines on track

Schedules tend to drift off track. Deadlines usually help with that. But some tools aren't supportive in dealing with it. That's why with Orchestra's task management tools, every remote fellow can stay on top of deadlines and track progress in real-time, so no task gets overlooked or delayed. 

Key features

  • Statuses in chat boards. No more micromanagement — stay on top of deadlines with a streamlined workspace that tracks task progress effortlessly. Task statuses are visible directly in the chat boards, displayed on each task icon, so you can monitor progress without leaving the conversation.
  • Statuses in the chat sidebar. Need more details? Simply open the chat sidebar to access all the important task information in one place, like whether a deadline is set, without interrupting your workflow.
  • Statuses in the projects section. View project statuses or individual tasks in via a list or Kanban — similar to Trello or ClickUp, but with more dynamic and interactive features. This gives you a more active and efficient way to manage projects and track progress.

Minimize distractions with focused communication solutions

Too much communication can drain productivity. Orchestra's solutions streamline all communication — whether it's messaging, calls, or updates — into one unified app. This helps reduce distractions, keeps conversations organized, and allows your team to focus on what truly matters, without the need to juggle between different tools.

Key Features

  • Integrated external users. Invite external contributors to any task, call, or chat without ever leaving the app. Our API solution facilitates smooth collaboration to keep everything in one place. No need to switch between platforms anymore.
  • Share team updates with external audiences. Want to extend your product's reach? Make your channel public for external users and ensure your updates are seen beyond your team’s internal workspace.
  • Task-specific chats for better focus. Instead of managing endless threads create dedicated chats for each task or project, ensuring your messages reach the right people without getting lost in unrelated discussions. Stay connected and organized while keeping your team on track.
💡
Pro tip. Encourage your team to use Orchestra's task commenting feature to provide updates and avoid messing up communication channels.

Boosting productivity in a remote team isn't just about working harder — it's about working smarter. With Orchestra, you get an all-in-one solution that combines crucial communication and task tools into a single platform: chats with files, calls with recordings, documents with attachments, custom fields with structured data, API integrations, etc. This allows you to experience seamless workflows and helps your team focus on what matters most.
