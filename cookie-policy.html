Cookies Policy

Orchestra employs cookies and similar technologies, including pixel tags, web beacons, clear GIFs, JavaScript, and local storage. These Cookies are small files, typically composed of letters and numbers, that are placed on your computer, tablet, phone, or similar device when you access our website. Throughout this Cookie Policy, we will collectively refer to these technologies as "Cookies."

Cookies are integral to our service, as they enable our servers to identify your web browser, track your visits to and usage of the Orchestra website, analyze trends, and gain insights into our user base.

We may combine non-personally identifiable information gathered through Cookies with other Personal Information we have about you, such as your identity or account status. Additionally, we may supplement the information collected from you with data obtained from third parties, either to enhance our Services or provide you with information we believe may be of interest to you. In cases where Cookies are used to collect personally identifiable information or information that may become personally identifiable when combined with other data, the Privacy Policy supplements this Cookie Policy.

You have the ability to control and delete Cookies at your discretion. For more information, please refer to aboutcookies.org. Most web browsers include settings that allow you to disable Cookies, thereby preventing your browser from accepting new Cookies or giving you options for accepting each new Cookie. You also have the option to delete any Cookies that are already stored on your computer, and most browsers provide settings to prevent future Cookie placement. However, please note that disabling Cookies may necessitate manual adjustments to your preferences each time you visit a website, and certain services and features may not function correctly. We strongly recommend keeping Cookies enabled to fully utilize Orchestra's features.

Essential Cookies

Essential Cookies are necessary to provide you with requested features or services, such as enabling you to log into secure areas of our site or utilize a shopping cart. Disabling these Cookies may impair the performance of our Services and render certain features and services unavailable.

Functionality Cookies

Functionality Cookies are employed to recognize you upon your return to our Services. This enables us to personalize our content, address you by name, and remember your preferences, such as language or region choices.

Performance/Analytical Cookies

Performance/Analytical Cookies help us comprehend how visitors use our Website and Service by collecting data on the number of visitors, pages viewed, and the duration of page visits. These Cookies also assist in evaluating the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns, contributing to enhancements in our campaigns and Service content for those who engage with our advertising.

Retargeting/Advertising Cookies

Retargeting/Advertising Cookies gather information about your online activity and identify your interests. This data enables us to deliver advertising that we believe is pertinent to you.

Third-Party Cookies

Third-Party Cookies are placed on your device by external parties and may convey information about your browsing habits, including visits to our Services, viewed pages, and clicked links. This information helps identify whether certain third-party services are in use, ascertains your interests, facilitates retargeting, and serves relevant advertisements. Please note that we lack control or access to these Cookies.

Session Cookies and Persistent Cookies

Cookies may be categorized as "session Cookies" or "persistent Cookies." Session Cookies are temporary and exist on your device only during your visit to our site, while persistent Cookies remain on your device for a specified period after you leave our site. Persistent Cookies serve to retain your preferences for subsequent visits and maintain accurate records of your engagement with our Services over time. They also contribute to measuring the effectiveness of our advertising endeavors. Through persistent Cookies, we may gather information about your online activity post-departure from our Services.

Please note that some web browsers provide a "Do Not Track" option, allowing you to signal your desire to opt out of being tracked for specific online activities across different websites and services, including behavioral advertising services. At present, our Services do not support "Do Not Track" requests, signifying that we collect information about your online activity while using our Services and following your departure.

Google Analytics Cookie and Usage

Our Services make use of Google Analytics, a web analysis service provided by Google Inc. ("Google") that relies on Cookie technology. Typically, the data generated by the Cookie is transmitted to a Google server in the United States, where it is stored. This website activates IP anonymization, causing your IP address to be shortened once received by Google. Google will employ the generated information on behalf of Orch Inc. to assess your website usage, compile reports on website activities, and furnish the website operator with additional services related to website and Internet utilization. Importantly, the IP address transmitted by your browser in connection with Google Analytics is not correlated with other data by Google.

To opt out of tracking by Google Analytics across all websites, you can visit http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
